# FlowAI 🚦

AI-Driven Urban Traffic Intelligence & Proactive Congestion Management System

Live Demo

https://ai-driven-urban-traffic-intelligenc.vercel.app/

FlowAI is an intelligent urban mobility platform designed to predict, simulate, and optimize city-wide traffic flow before congestion occurs.

Unlike traditional navigation applications that react to existing traffic conditions, FlowAI takes a proactive approach by forecasting congestion hotspots, simulating traffic scenarios, and recommending adaptive interventions that improve traffic efficiency across the entire road network.

---

Problem Statement

Urban traffic congestion remains one of the most critical challenges in rapidly growing cities. Existing navigation systems primarily focus on individual route optimization, often shifting congestion from one area to another rather than solving it at a network level.

Major challenges include:

* Increasing travel delays
* Fuel wastage due to stop-and-go traffic
* Rising carbon emissions
* Delayed emergency vehicle response
* Limited ability to simulate future traffic conditions

FlowAI addresses these challenges through predictive intelligence, digital twin simulations, and adaptive optimization strategies.

 Core Features

#### Spatio-Temporal Congestion Forecasting

FlowAI models urban traffic as a dynamic network and predicts future congestion hotspots using graph-based traffic intelligence.

Features:

* Future congestion prediction
* Traffic demand forecasting
* Risk-based hotspot detection
* Congestion heatmaps
