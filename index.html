The Gridlock Crisis

Urban Congestion is a System Failure

Traditional navigation apps are reactive. They route individual cars onto the exact same bypasses, shifting traffic from one gridlock to another. The result? A cascade of compounding delays.

How We Solve It

Wasted Travel Time

Commuters waste an average of 54 hours per year stuck in gridlocks, causing massive productivity losses.

Excess Fuel Burn

Stop-and-go driving burns millions of gallons of excess fuel, costing vehicle owners billions annually.

Soot & Pollution

Idling vehicles generate excessive localized emissions, deteriorating air quality in residential corridors.

Delayed Responders

Congested intersections block emergency vehicles, directly impacting critical patient response times.

AI Architecture

Spatio-Temporal Graph Neural Networks

Cities are graphs. Intersections are nodes; roads are edges. Our custom GNN models understand the flow of traffic not just as local street blocks, but as highly interconnected spatial systems propagating congestion through time.

  • Models spatial propagation (how jams spread)
  • Forecasts future states up to 45 minutes ahead
  • Fuses spatial layout with temporal cyclical patterns
Adaptive Optimization

Reinforcement Learning Signal Control

Static timers fail under pressure. FlowAI deploys cooperative, multi-agent reinforcement learning at every intersection. Signal controllers communicate with neighbors, dynamically adapting green times to keep traffic moving smoothly.

  • Self-learning agents adapt to changing demand profiles
  • Neighbor-to-neighbor cooperative corridor syncing
  • Priority corridors for transit and emergency vehicles
